512 We are excited to be your reliable partner during the recruitment process. Together we are transforming the way we recruit today, by making it simpler and more effective for both you and your candidates. Our team listens to your concerns and works continuously to solve your daily work hassles. 
513
514 Catch a glimpse of SmartCV’s new features, that will better serve you and your team:
515
516 Send bulk emails to candidates
517 Do you want to contact multiple candidates at once? Are you tired of sending one email after the other? Well, you don’t have to anymore. All you need to do is simply select the candidates and corresponding email template that matches your purpose. Formal or informal greeting? We’ve got you covered, SmartCV adjusts the greeting in Greek - (Αγαπητή Ελένη, Αγαπητέ κύριε Ιωαννίδη).
518
519 Download candidates data in excel
520 Get the structured data we collect from candidates in an excel file based on job, organization, or as a complete overview of all applicants and use it according to your preferences whilst gaining key insights that will level up your hiring strategy.
521
522 Give every applicant a fair chance through blind hiring
523 As stated in a study of PwC in 2017, 1 in 5 women experienced gender discrimination during the recruitment procecess, in comparison to 5% of men. We optionally remove information like name, gender, and university names from candidate’s CVs in an attempt to mitigate unconscious bias.
524
525 Attach files such as academic certificates to the candidate’s application
526 Remember that time you were searching for a specific document and had to look through all of your email threads? No more tedious scrolling through your inbox to find relevant documents - keep them all safely organized in one place, the candidate’s profile.
527
528 Create interview kits for consistent and accurate screening
529 Having difficulties comparing candidates’ answers? With SmartCV you have a structure at hand - ask candidates the same questions, score their answers on a scale, and add your notes instantly.
530
531 Benefit from custom workflows
532 Do you wish to follow a linear recruiting approach, in which candidates are moved from one stage to the next or a more flexible one that allows skipping hiring stages? Either way is fine with us! Did one of your colleagues interview a candidate and give them the green light? We make sure you won’t miss a thing - get notifications whenever a candidate is moved to another hiring stage.
